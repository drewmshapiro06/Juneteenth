Daytime Flow

12:00 — 12:20 PM HEADS UP
PETER

Peter is getting his haircut

Heads up for everyone — Peter has a haircut appointment from 12:00 to 12:20. He'll meet us at the court right after. Don't start the real run without him. Use the extra time to warm up, stretch, or grab water.

WAIT FOR PETE
12:30 PM ACTIVITY 01

Basketball — Tip Off

Meet at the court by 12:30. Pete should be there by then. We've got the court for a little over an hour. Bring water, you'll need it.

  • Run full court if we have the numbers
  • Keep teams fair — switch up after every few games
  • Last game wraps by 1:40 so we can roll out clean
1:45 PM ACTIVITY 02

Lunch — Together

Everyone eats as a group right after hoops. Pick a spot close to the court so we're not driving all over. Fast and casual — we want to be back on the move by 2:45 latest. Talk it out and pick the spot the morning of.

2:50 PM PIT STOP

Stop for Drinks

On the way to Jake's, we swing by the liquor store. A few different people will be making runs throughout the day, so don't stress about getting everything in one trip. Grab enough for the afternoon, plus ice and mixers.

Someone also bring a cooler to Jake's so we can keep everything cold without running back and forth to the fridge.

3:00 PM HOME BASE

Jake's House

Set up at Jake's for the afternoon. This is home base for the rest of the day. Get changed, get comfortable, and let the day settle in.

From 3 onward we're playing dye, hanging out, swimming, and just kicking it. Music going, drinks going, no real schedule until we hit the 7 PM decision point.

3:15 — 7:00 PM ACTIVITY 03

Dye + Pool + Hang

Long afternoon block. Dye in the backyard, pool when we need to cool off, drinks the whole time. Take it slow — this is the longest stretch of the day and we want everyone in good shape by dinner.

  • Pace the drinking — long way to go
  • Food and water on rotation
  • Cooler keeps the drinks cold all afternoon
02

The 7 PM Decision

DECISION POINT — 7:00 PM

How does the night go?

Three options. We figure it out whenever, based on how everyone's feeling.

OPTION A
STAY AT JAKE'S

Lock in at Jake's

Nobody leaves. We keep the night going right here. Either order food in or fire up the grill and do BBQ. Change out of swim stuff if needed.

  • BBQ or delivery — group call
  • Change of clothes from the pool
  • Lowest effort, most chill
  • Bong might make an appearance later for whoever's in
OPTION B
HOME → REGROUP LATE

Home, Then Out

Everyone heads home after dye, showers, gets cleaned up. Then we link back up at night for whatever the move is. Doesn't have to be at Jake's.

  • Reset, shower, eat something small
  • Pick a new spot in the group chat
  • Best if we want to actually go out
OPTION C
HOME → LATE DINNER

Late Group Dinner

Same as B but lower energy. Everyone goes home, showers, then we all meet up later for a proper sit-down dinner. Reservation if needed.

  • Pick a spot by 6 so we can book
  • Real food, real seats
  • Easy way to end the day right
